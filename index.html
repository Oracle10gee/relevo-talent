About Us

The Problem

Frontier Asian and European countries present immense potential for growth and opportunities. These nations have young and growing populations well-positioned to capitalize on the demographic dividend. With a growing middle class, urbanization, and the adoption of new technology, there is a rising demand for goods and services. However, they still face challenges that require expert attention.

Advanced countries are pushing production and manufacturing to frontier countries due to cost inflation, while frontier countries offer tax incentives, lower manufacturing costs, and diversification away from concentrated China risk.

Frontier nations also have high rates of economic growth and large, increasingly wealthy addressable markets. Companies can put production closer to their customers in these new markets, and businesses from more mature economies as they seek new growth opportunities.

In summary, frontier countries present an opportunity for growth, diversification, and cost savings, and we are experts in navigating the unique challenges and opportunities they offer by helping businesses in these countries meet the labour resource needs.

HOW WE ARE SOLVING THE PROBLEM

We solve the challenges that our clients face with recruitment and immigration. We offer our clients an all-in-one solution to help them connect with skilled overseas staff.

As an exclusive talent consulting company, we provide integrated recruitment, immigration, and settlement solutions to assist employers in the 24 frontier countries and facilitate the employment of skilled migrants, making the process efficient and straightforward for everyone involved.

Our registered migration agents are committed to ensuring that every application is processed correctly, simplifying the migration and visa processing experience for both the employer and the talent. This ensures that all relevant government norms are fulfilled, making the migration process simple, compliant, and stress-free.

With our agency, you can be rest assured that you are in good hands. We are dedicated to connecting talent with opportunity, bridging the gap between skilled overseas staff and forward-thinking businesses across Asia and Europe.

Here are the eight main components of the Talent Matching Process

1. Visa and Migration Assessment: There is no need starting a job suitability process if the chances of getting you an immigration visa is slim. Thus, our first step is to assess you based on your basic information, travel history, and other records (Criminal, Social) for suitability of a candidate. We process over 1000 candidates monthly and we would disappoint to start a process that cannot be completed because of migration challenges. We have a number of expert migration consultants who have merged the requirements of all the countries our clients are located into one methodology to test for ease of migration.

2. Professional Fit: Here, we assess the suitability of interested candidates in terms of the educational qualification , skill set and work experience. While educational qualification is a core requirement for this, we place high rating of candidates’ skills, professional qualification and practical experiences on the job. It is in this stage we match candidates with sectors and openings across our database of labour resource needs of our clients.

3. Document Verification: Once we have finalised the professional fit, you would be contacted to upload all necessary credentials and basic documents via our secured application portal. This should include a reference letter from financial institution, mortgage provider, or any verifiable institution they belong to.

4. Job Application: As a next step, a standardized resume for the applicant will be created and sent to the profiled employers along with the credentials. We do not post job ads to candidates. Instead, we apply on the candidate's behalf to the 150 businesses we work for. Our recruitment team is experienced in verifying overseas qualifications, reference checking, and interviewing migrant candidates to ensure they best suit our client's needs.

5. Processing: We engage with employers during the application process and collaborate with them on the review of terms and benefits. This stage involves a waiting period of up to 60 days.

6. Review and Acceptance of employment offer: All job offers would be discussed with the applicant, and we would guide them in making a choice.

7. Employment Contract: The employer formalizes the job offer with a standard employment contract and an expatriate request to the Department of Immigration and Citizenship.

8. Visa and Migration Service: An appointed migration consultant will work with the applicant to finalize the visa for them and their dependents, as well as accommodations and any other necessary benefits that must be signed up for.
